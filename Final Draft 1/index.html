Introducing the

Claflin University Smart Home System



The aim of this project is to design a smart home system that integrates various smart devices and sensors to improve the quality of life for homeowners. The system will be designed to provide users with easy control of home appliances, lighting, temperature, security, and other devices using their smartphones or other devices. To accomplish this, we will implement a combination of hardware and software technologies, including Internet of Things (IoT), machine learning, and cloud computing.
The system will use sensors and cameras to gather data about the home environment, analyze this data to make decisions about device control, and use cloud computing to store and process data.

ROUTINES

Automate your home

Google Routines are a feature within the Google Assistant ecosystem that allows users to automate tasks with a single command. Users can create personalized routines, defining trigger phrases or times to activate a sequence of actions. For example, a "Good Morning" routine can gradually increase lights, play a radio station, and provide weather forecasts. Another routine, "Bedtime," can lower the thermostat, turn off lights, and play soothing music. Routines can also be location-based, triggering actions upon arrival or departure.
